Homewares Buying Admin Assistant

While our Buyers continue to source new brands and collections to keep our brands on trend and unique to our competitors, there is an urgency to ensure that these products are available on our site and to our couples as soon as possible. The successful candidate will have a keen eye for detail and will have advanced MS Excel skills that enable them to help manage our product database and ensure pricing, margins and product updates are accurately and efficiently updated.

    Job Requirements
  • Maintaining product, brands and collection changes onto our internal back-end system in a timely manner, employing impeccable attention to detail in relation to all product information
  • Liaising with suppliers to request price updates, ensuring prices are in line with agreed terms
  • Accurately updating all price changes and product updates or discontinuations via our internal back-end system in a timely manner and responding to any pricing issues across the business
  • Management and troubleshooting of any invoice discrepancies between our Finance team and Suppliers to mitigate any margin erosion
  • Assisting the Buying team to strategically manage the reorganisation of the product database for commercial reasons
  • Owning the database quality management process, carrying out spot-checks and running reports to identify and rectify any pricing, product information or upload errors
  • Creating KPI reports for the Buying team as requested
