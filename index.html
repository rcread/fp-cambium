About Us

The Wedding Shop launched in 1990, the UK's longest established independent gift list. Gary Channon aquired TWS in 2015 and appointed Anne-Marie Jenkins as CEO. The business grew tenfold in 5 years and The Wedding Present Company (formerly Wedding Presents Direct) was acquired to create Wedding List Solutions.The Cambium Group was formed in 2019 when Wedding List Solutions acquired Prezola and Rock My Wedding. Since then The Homeware Outlet has been launched,25% of Hostology was acquired and we are expecting the arrival of our baby list company, Little List to launch in early 2023.

Little List

Little List will offer a unique proposition that captures the growing trend for baby showers and baby gifting, as well as giving new parents a one-stop shop to find products themselves. LittleList uses fun and clever online tools so expecting parents know what they will actually need for their baby (since this can be so different according to lifestyle). Then it allows friends to offer up their own used items against what they will need.

Prezola

Prezola is the UK’s favourite wedding gift list. Create your online gift list and join thousands of happy couples. A Prezola gift list is the easy way for guests to buy you a wedding gift. You create a list of things you love and share it with your guests. We'll keep track of what everyone's bought and deliver the gifts to you after your wedding.
